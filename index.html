Money Tracker
Version 1.0.0
Money Tracker is a simple personal finance app designed to help you track your monthly income, expenses, and remaining balance in one place.

You can add your income, record daily expenses, and instantly see how your money is being spent throughout the month. The app automatically updates your balance so you always know your current financial situation.

Built especially for students and bachelors who often lose track of where their money goes — Money Tracker helps build better financial habits with clear and simple tracking.
Features
✓ Add income and funds
✓ Track daily expenses
✓ Auto balance calculation
✓ Full transaction history
